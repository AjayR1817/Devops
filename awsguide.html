How to actually use this

For each video: watch it, then write a 2–3 sentence summary in your own words before checking it off. If you can't summarize it, you didn't learn it yet — rewatch.

Do the mini-project at the end of each phase before moving on. The exam tests architecture decisions, not video-watching — touching the console is what makes it stick.

If you finish a phase and can't answer its checkpoint without looking anything up, that phase isn't done. Go back before moving forward.

  • Phase 00–01 (identity & setup): go slow — almost everything later assumes you have this cold
  • Phase 06 & 08 (security, VPC): the densest phases — budget extra time, don't rush them
  • Phase 11 (practice exams): space the 4 tests out by at least a few hours each, and actually review every wrong answer's explanation
